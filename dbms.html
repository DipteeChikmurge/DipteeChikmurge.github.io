Introduction to Agentic AI

Agentic AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that possess the ability to autonomously make decisions, plan, and execute tasks with minimal human intervention. These systems are designed to exhibit high levels of adaptability, goal-setting, and reasoning capabilities.

Key Characteristics

  • Autonomous decision-making
  • Self-learning and adaptation
  • Proactive goal setting
  • Interactive reasoning
  • Context awareness

Applications

  • Robotics and automation
  • AI-driven virtual assistants
  • Autonomous vehicles
  • Financial market predictions
  • Healthcare diagnostics
